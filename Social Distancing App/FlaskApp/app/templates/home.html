Welcome to the Social Distancing Detection App

The Covid-19 pandemic has shifted how we work and socialize with each other, non-pharmaceutical interventions have thus far played an important role in stopping the spread of the virus, one of these measures is social distancing. Social distancing so far has proven to be an effective method in controlling the virus, however it can be difficult to see how often social distancing has been violated in a particular scene or at a particular time. This app provides a method to track this data so that users can adjust how they transverse a given area.

How to make a detection ?

To monitor social distancing in real-time, you must first register for an account or login if you already have an existing account, once this is done proceed to navigate toward the new detection page, in the new detection page there will be a live-video feed showing the current area your webcam is pointing to. Once you are satisfied with your detection, click the end detection button. You can navigate to the detections and detection graph sections to see how many violations have occurred overtime during your sessions.

How the app works ?

The app works by using a neural network on the real-time video feed gained from your web cam, the video stream is processed to detect the number of people within a scene and the distance between them, if people within a scene are considered to be violating social distancing guidelines a red bounding-box is place between them.
